DiveEdit first-run setup needs free disk space for external runtime assets.

Recommended before continuing:

- CPU-only computers: at least 6 GB free on the Windows user/cache drive.
- NVIDIA GPU computers: at least 10 GB free, because setup may download CUDA/cuDNN runtime files and a Whisper speech model.

Setup may download:

- ffmpeg video tools
- CUDA/cuDNN runtime files for GPU acceleration, when an NVIDIA GPU is detected
- a faster-whisper speech model

These assets are cached so reinstalling is faster. If the computer has limited space, free disk space first or skip first-run setup and prepare the assets later.
